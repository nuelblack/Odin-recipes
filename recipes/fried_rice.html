Fried Rice Recipe

This is one of Nigeria's staple meal. It is very rich in fiber, carbonhydrates, vitamins and proteins, and a very rich list of benefits that will be discussed later below.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Groundnut or olive oil
  • Pepper (fresh or grinded)
  • Green Peas
  • Spring onions
  • Baked beans and sweet corn
  • Bell peppers
  • Spices
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Protein of choice
  • Ginger and Garlic

Steps to prepare this meal:

  1. Wash and boil preferred protein with spices
  2. moderately fry the protein and reserve the meat stock
  3. parboil the rice
  4. Fry the chopped pepper, peas, onions, diced liver etc. in the oil
  5. Use the prepared fries to stir fry the already parboiled rice
  6. Add the meat stock and a little water to the stir fried rice and wait until it simmers and mixes well enough and loses all the added water.

Hoorah!!! Fried Rice should be ready in no time. There are amazing sides that can complement the oil_beans and this includes and is not limited to the following;

  • Fried Plantains
  • Salads
  • Cold slaw
  • Boiled or fried Eggs
