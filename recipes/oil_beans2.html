Oil Beans Recipe

This is one of Nigeria's staple meal. It is very rich in fiber and carbonhydrate and proteins and also has a rich list of benefits that will be discussed later below.

Ingredients:

  • Beans
  • Water
  • Palm oil
  • Pepper (fresh or grinded)
  • Spices
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps to prepare this meal:

  1. Clean and sift the beans from dirts
  2. Boil beans in water until it softens
  3. Add some salt and spices to the soften beans
  4. Use a separate pot to bleach the palm oil slightly
  5. Fry your fresh pepper until it's ready
  6. Add the prepared pepper into the boiling beans and wait until it simmers and mixes well enough.

Hoorah!!! Beans should be ready in 10 minutes after oil is added to the mix and one can eat as soon as possible. There are amazing sides that can complement the oil_beans and this includes and is not limited to the following;

  • Fried or boiled Plantains
  • Boiled Yam
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Pap or custard
  • Garri (As we locally call it here made from Cassava)
