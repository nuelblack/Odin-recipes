Jollof Rice Recipe

This is one of Nigeria's staple meal. It is very rich in fiber and carbonhydrate, and also has a rich list of benefits that will be discussed later in this article.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Groundnut or olive oil
  • Pepper (fresh or grinded)
  • Spices
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Protein of choice
  • Ginger and Garlic

Steps to prepare this meal:

  1. Wash and boil preferred protein with spices
  2. Mildly fry the protein and reserve the meat stuck
  3. parboil the rice
  4. Fry the pepper in the oil and add the meat stock reserved for taste
  5. Add the prepared pepper into the boiling rice and wait until it simmers and mixes well enough and loses all it's water.

Hoorah!!! Smokey Jollof Rice is ready when it tastes properly cooked to the right texture and has lost its water. There are amazing sides that can complement the oil_beans and this includes and is not limited to the following;

  • Fried Plantains
  • Salad
  • Cold slaw
  • Boiled Eggs
